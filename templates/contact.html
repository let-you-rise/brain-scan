Frequently Asked Questions

Is CardioScan AI a replacement for medical diagnosis?

No, CardioScan AI is designed as a preliminary screening tool to help identify potential risks. It should not replace professional medical evaluation. Always consult with healthcare professionals for proper diagnosis and treatment.

How accurate is the heart disease prediction?

Our model achieves approximately 98% accuracy in controlled testing environments. However, accuracy may vary based on image quality and individual factors. Results should be considered indicative rather than definitive.

Is my medical data stored or shared?

We prioritize your privacy. Uploaded images are processed securely and are not stored on our servers after analysis is complete. We do not share any personal medical information with third parties.

What types of images work best with CardioScan AI?

CardioScan AI is optimized for cardiac MRI images in JPG format. For best results, use high-resolution images taken by professional medical equipment.

How can healthcare providers integrate with CardioScan AI?

We offer API integration options for healthcare providers. Please contact our business development team at partners@cardioscan.ai for more information about partnerships and integration.

Is CardioScan AI available worldwide?

Yes, our web platform is accessible globally. However, regulatory approval may vary by country. Please check local regulations regarding the use of AI-assisted medical tools in your region.
