About CardioScan AI

Learn about our mission, technology, and the development journey behind our heart disease prediction platform

Our Mission

At CardioScan AI, our mission is to leverage artificial intelligence to democratize access to cardiac health assessments, enabling early detection of heart disease and ultimately saving lives.

We believe that combining cutting-edge AI technology with medical imaging can create a powerful tool for both healthcare professionals and individuals concerned about their heart health.

By providing an accessible platform for preliminary heart disease risk assessment, we aim to encourage more people to take proactive steps towards heart health and seek appropriate medical care when needed.

Our Technology

Deep Learning

Our AI model uses convolutional neural networks (CNNs) trained on thousands of cardiac MRI images to identify patterns associated with heart disease.

Image Processing

Advanced image preprocessing techniques ensure that uploaded cardiac images are properly analyzed regardless of source or format variations.

Secure Processing

We prioritize privacy with secure image processing that doesn't store your medical data, ensuring confidentiality and compliance with health data regulations.

Note: CardioScan AI is designed as a preliminary screening tool and should not replace professional medical diagnosis. Always consult with healthcare professionals for proper medical evaluation.

Development Journey

2025 Q1

Research Phase

Began researching the application of deep learning to cardiac imaging and assembled our initial dataset of cardiac MRI images.

2025 Q2

Model Development

Developed and trained our first CNN model on 10,000+ cardiac images to identify patterns associated with heart disease.

2025 Q3

Algorithm Refinement

Refined our algorithms and improved accuracy through collaboration with cardiology experts and additional training data.

2025 Q4

Beta Testing

Launched beta testing with select healthcare partners to validate our technology in real-world environments.

Present

Continuous Improvement

Ongoing research and development to improve accuracy, add new features, and expand our platform's capabilities.
